Environmental Commitement

At BMML, we are deeply committed to the philosophy of Gross National Happiness (GNH), which guides our approach to business and environmental stewardship. Preservation of the environment is a cornerstone of GNH, and we fully comply with all environmental policies set forth by the Royal Government of Bhutan.
