About BMML

Background

Established in 2024, BMML Company has quickly emerged as a leading producer of high-quality ferro silicon. Strategically located in Bhutan, our company leverages the region's abundant natural resources and consistent power supply to deliver exceptional products to our global clientele. With a dedicated team and state-of-the-art facilities, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of production and sustainability, ensuring that we meet the diverse needs of industries worldwide.
